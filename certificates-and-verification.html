Purpose

Why certification matters

Your certificate turns a score into portable proof. It states your CEFR level and gives decision‑makers a secure way to verify authenticity and validity in real time.

  • Clarity — concise fields that admissions and HR teams can trust immediately.
  • Security — unique Certificate ID, link, and QR provide instant verification.
Scope

Where it’s used

  • Admissions and placement
  • Hiring and promotion
  • Licensing and compliance
  • Personal and global mobility
Certificate

What your certificate shows

CEFR level

Overall CEFR outcome (A1–C2); concise skill insight may be included where relevant.

Identity & issue details

Candidate name, test date, issue date, and validity period (2 years).

Verification fields

Unique Certificate ID with a shareable link and QR code for instant verification.

On the day

Test‑day guidance & live support

  • Check in Verify ID and confirm approved accommodations with the proctor.
