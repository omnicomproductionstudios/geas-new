Purpose

What the exam is

Proficia certifies practical communication. Tasks mirror authentic contexts—emails, briefings, short reports, and conversations—so results reflect how English is used in universities and workplaces, not just textbook recall.

  • Real-world tasks — scenario-based prompts designed around purpose and audience.
  • Clear outcomes — results mapped to CEFR (A1–C2) for portability.
Scope

Where it’s used

  • Admissions and placement
  • Hiring and promotion
  • Professional licensing and compliance
  • Personal and global mobility

For candidates

Students, professionals, and applicants who need a trusted, portable proof of English ability.

  • Single sitting — complete in ~90–120 minutes.
  • Fast results — issued within 7 working days.
  • Verifiable — digital certificate with unique ID.
  • Accessible — 100% online with live proctoring.

For institutions & employers

A clear, CEFR-aligned indicator of English ability with quick verification.

  • CEFR A1–C2 reporting for easy interpretation
  • Consistent scoring using calibrated methods
  • Shareable verification link and audit trail

How it works

  • Book & prepare Choose a date, check your system, and review the test-day guide.
  • ID & proctor check Verify identity and complete a short environment scan before starting.
  • Complete the exam Follow the guided flow across Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking.
  • Results & certificate Receive your CEFR result within 7 working days and a verifiable digital certificate.

What it measures

Four core skills assessed through concise, scenario-based tasks. Each task targets purpose, audience, and clarity so performance reflects real-life use of English.

Reading

Extract main ideas, detail, and inference from applied texts and reports.

Listening

Understand multi-accent audio: briefings, interviews, short lectures.

Writing

Produce clear emails, summaries, and short analyses with appropriate tone.

Speaking

Respond to interview-style prompts; clarity, coherence, and lexical control.
