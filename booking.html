Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to help you understand the exam and booking procedures better

Is the exam fully online?

Yes, it’s delivered 100% online with secure live proctoring, ID verification, and environment checks.

Is the exam fully online?

Yes, it’s delivered 100% online with secure live proctoring, ID verification, and environment checks.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes, within the policy window displayed during checkout. Rescheduling fees may apply.

What ID is required?

A valid passport or national ID matching your booking name is required to sit the exam.

What are the system requirements?

Use a desktop or laptop with a camera, microphone, and speakers; a stable internet connection; and a modern browser.

Do you offer accommodations?

Yes. You can request special accommodations during booking with relevant supporting documentation.

Can I request a re-evaluation?

Yes, within five days of receiving your results. A review fee may apply, and all re-evaluations are final.

Can I retake the exam?

Yes. Retakes are available after seven days, allowing you to improve your score.

Is the exam accredited?

PROFICIA follows CEFR standards, but recognition and acceptance depend on each organization’s policy.

Where can institutions verify my results?

Every certificate includes a secure verification link and unique ID for instant authenticity checks.
