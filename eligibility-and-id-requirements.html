The Exam

Eligibility & ID Requirements

Check eligibility and prepare the correct identity documents to ensure a smooth, secure test day. Your booking name must match your ID exactly.

Who can take the exam Accepted ID

At glance

  • Minimum age: 16+ (under 16 by request)
  • ID name must match booking
  • Valid, government‑issued photo ID
  • Webcam + microphone required
Start Booking
Purpose

Why requirements matter

Clear eligibility and ID rules protect fairness for every candidate and ensure results can be trusted by universities and employers worldwide.

  • Integrity — verified identity and presence throughout the exam session.
  • Portability — documentation that decision‑makers can accept without additional checks.
Scope

Where it applies

  • All test locations worldwide
  • All delivery modes (remote proctoring)
  • All candidates and retakes
Verification

Name matching & capture

  • Your booking name must match your ID exactly (including middle names and diacritics, where applicable)
  • On test day, show the physical ID to the camera when prompted by the proctor.
  • We capture a live photo and short video for presence verification and audit.
  • Name change? Provide official proof (e.g., marriage certificate, court order) before test day.
Security

Live checks

Lighting should clearly show your face and the ID. Do not use filters or screen‑sharing tools.

Checklist

Before you book

Valid ID ready

Have a passport, national ID, or driver’s license that matches your name exactly.

Quiet room

Private space with adequate lighting and no background voices.

Device & browser

Laptop/desktop with webcam and mic; use a modern browser with updates installed.

Connection

Stable internet; we recommend a wired or strong Wi‑Fi connection.

Policies

Review Candidate Terms, Privacy Policy, and Exam Rules; agree during checkout.

Time window

Allow ~120 minutes uninterrupted for setup and the exam itself.

Special cases

Additional guidance

Minors (under 16)

Testing is possible by request with guardian consent and approved identification. Contact support before booking.

Name changes

If your legal name recently changed, submit official proof at least 48 hours before your slot for update approval.

Unsupported documents

Use wired headphones and adjust volume levels. Captions are available for instructions where appropriate.
