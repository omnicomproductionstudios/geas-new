Why Choose Proficia

Proficia was created to set a new standard in English proficiency testing. Every aspect of the platform is built to ensure precision, fairness, and global accessibility. From our CEFR-aligned scoring to secure online delivery, we combine human expertise with advanced technology to give candidates, employers, and institutions results they can trust.

Learn More
01

CEFR-aligned results

Our reporting follows the Common European Framework of Reference (A1 to C2), giving candidates a globally recognized measure of proficiency. Each score clearly reflects communication ability and can be easily interpreted by universities, employers, and public institutions.

02

Real-world assessment

Proficia evaluates how English is used in everyday, academic, and professional contexts. Candidates complete realistic, scenario-based tasks that test genuine communication rather than memorized responses.

03

Integrity by design

Security is central to our process. Each session includes identity verification, live remote proctoring, and continuous monitoring to maintain exam authenticity and eliminate misconduct.

04

Consistent outcomes

Every result is reviewed through calibrated scoring systems and expert evaluators. This ensures accuracy, reliability, and fairness across all candidate profiles and testing sessions.

05

Fast, verifiable certification

Results are issued within seven working days, accompanied by a secure digital certificate. Each document includes a unique verification ID and shareable link for easy validation worldwide.

06

Global access

Proficia is entirely online, allowing candidates to schedule and complete their exam from anywhere in the world. Our platform is optimized for flexibility, accessibility, and seamless user experience across devices and time zones.
