Our Exam at Glance

01

What we Measure

PROFICIA certifies real world English skills through practical, scenario based tasks that measure how you read, listen, write, and speak. Each task aligns with CEFR standards to ensure clear, reliable, and globally comparable results.

02

Exam Options

  • General Exam - Tests everyday communication for work or daily use.
  • Academic Exam - Focuses on academic and university-level English.
  • Custom Exam - Tailored for organizations or institutions with specific needs.

03

Skills Assessed

  • Reading – Identify key ideas, details, and inferences in texts and reports.
  • Listening – Comprehend multi-accent audio from talks, interviews, and briefings.
  • Writing – Create clear, structured responses with accurate tone and grammar.
  • Speaking – Express ideas clearly and naturally in interview-style tasks.

04

Format

  • Format – One guided session testing all four skills.
  • Tasks – Real scenarios with clear, concise prompts.
  • Flow – Seamless progression, no long pauses.
  • Time – Around 90 to 120 minutes.

05

Delivery

  • Delivery – Online with live remote proctoring.
  • Verification – ID and environment checks before the exam.
  • Monitoring – Continuous supervision and task variation.
  • Integrity – Strict controls to ensure fairness and privacy.

06

Scoring & Results

  • Hybrid scoring: calibrated technology + trained evaluators
  • Outcomes reported as a single CEFR level (A1–C2), with sub-skill insight where relevant
  • Results issued within 14 working days and posted to your dashboard

CEFR Alignment

Results use the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) to provide a shared international reference for levels—so institutions and employers can interpret outcomes at a glance.

  • Results follow the CEFR scale for global consistency.
  • Institutions and employers can interpret levels instantly.

Certificate & Verification

Receive a secure digital certificate valid for two years, complete with a unique verification ID for easy authentication and global recognition.

  • Instantly verifiable through a secure online link
  • Accepted for study, employment, and professional use

Eligibility & ID

Proficia exams are open to candidates worldwide, with standard identification and age requirements to ensure secure, verified participation.

  • Recommended for ages 16 and above (parental consent if under 18)
  • Valid passport or national ID required for verification

System Requirements & Environment

Ensure your setup meets Proficia’s technical and environmental standards for a smooth, secure testing experience.

  • Use a desktop or laptop with a camera, microphone, and stable internet connection
  • Take the exam in a quiet, well-lit space with no assistance or distractions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers that help you understand our services better

The Proficia Exam is a CEFR-aligned English proficiency assessment designed to evaluate reading, listening, writing, and speaking skills through practical, real-world tasks.

The exam is open to candidates aged 16 and above, available globally except in regions restricted by local regulations.

Candidates can choose between the General, Academic, or Custom exams, depending on whether the goal is employment, study, or organizational testing.

The exam is conducted 100% online with live remote proctoring, identity verification, and environment checks to ensure fairness and integrity.

Most candidates complete the full four-skill assessment in approximately 90–120 minutes in a single guided sitting.

Results are typically issued within fourteen working days and appear in your Proficia dashboard with detailed CEFR-level reporting.

Proficia uses a hybrid model with calibrated AI technology combined with expert human evaluators to ensure accurate, consistent results.

Each candidate receives a digital certificate valid for two years, featuring a unique verification ID and secure link for third-party confirmation.
