What Drives Us

At Proficia, we believe that language should open doors and never create barriers. Our goal is to make English certification fair, accessible, and focused on real communication instead of memorized answers. We exist to help people prove their true ability with confidence wherever life takes them. Every feature we design is built to measure English as it is used in the real world with honesty and precision.

Vision

To become the world’s most trusted and advanced English proficiency platform, setting the benchmark for reliability, fairness, and accuracy.

Mission

To empower individuals through a fair, professional, and globally recognized English certification that is accessible from anywhere in the world.

Approach

We apply rigorous assessment design, calibrated scoring, and expert review to ensure every result reflects genuine English ability.

Values

We stand for fairness, integrity, and innovation. Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to honesty, excellence, and the belief that every candidate deserves a truly equal opportunity to succeed.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to make English certification meaningful and empowering. We want every candidate to feel seen, supported, and accurately represented through a process that values real communication over memorization.

Philosophy

We measure English as it exists in life, study, and work. Our philosophy is grounded in authenticity, transparency, and respect for each learner’s background and context.

Commitment

We are committed to maintaining excellence in every detail, from test design to support. Every feature we offer is carefully built to ensure fairness, security, and global consistency.

Impact

Proficia is more than a test. It is a bridge to opportunity for students, professionals, and institutions worldwide who rely on English to connect, grow, and succeed.

Future

We continue to innovate with advanced assessment technology, research partnerships, and AI-enhanced evaluation. Our goal is to redefine what English certification means for the next generation of global communicators.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers that help you understand our services better

What is the Proficia English Exam?

Proficia is a CEFR-aligned English proficiency test that measures real-world communication skills in reading, writing, listening, and speaking.

How is the exam delivered?

The exam is taken fully online with secure proctoring, ID verification, and AI monitoring to ensure fairness.

Who can take the exam?

Anyone aged 16 or older can register. It’s designed for students, professionals, and organizations that need verified certification.

How long does the exam take?

The full test takes about 90 to 120 minutes, depending on the chosen modules.

When will I receive my results?

Results are issued within 10 to 14 working days, along with a digital certificate containing a unique verification ID.

What standards does Proficia follow?

Proficia follows the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) to ensure transparent, internationally comparable results.

Can employers or institutions verify results?

Yes. Each certificate includes a secure online verification link for instant validation.

What kind of support is available?

Our team provides complete support before, during, and after your exam to ensure a smooth experience.
