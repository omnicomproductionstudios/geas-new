Purpose

Fair access without compromising integrity

Accommodations adapt delivery—not standards. Scoring criteria remain identical. Our goal is to remove barriers while keeping results comparable.

  • Candidate‑first — clear steps, predictable timelines, and respectful handling.
  • Consistency — solutions that preserve the validity of your result.
Scope

Who can apply

  • Long‑term disabilities or health conditions
  • Temporary injuries affecting test performance
  • Learning differences with professional documentation

Documentation

What to include

Professional statement

Diagnosis/condition, functional impact on testing, and recommended accommodations.

Recency

Provide the most recent relevant documentation to reflect your current needs.

Contact details

Include practitioner credentials and contact info for verification if required.

Timelines

  • Submit requests ≥ 10 business days before your preferred date.
  • We aim to confirm within 5–7 business days of receiving a complete file.
  • Results are issued within 14 calendar days after testing.
  • Accessible — 100% online with live proctoring.
